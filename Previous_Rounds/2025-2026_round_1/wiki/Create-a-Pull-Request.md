Once that your submission is uploaded to your fork, you can create a pull request to propose changes you've made.

1. Move to your fork main folder and click on `Contribute / Open pull request` button.
[[images/create_pr/contribute_open_pr.png]]

1. Make sure that the `base repository` is the main repository and the `head repository` is your fork.
[[images/create_pr/compare.png]]

1. Enter as title of the PR your team name, model name and and round_id, and then click on `Create pull request` button.
[[images/create_pr/title_create_pr.png]]
