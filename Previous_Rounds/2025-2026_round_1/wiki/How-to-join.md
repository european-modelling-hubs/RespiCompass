We welcome the active involvement of any group, individual, or institution interested in contributing to RespiCompass by providing scenario projections on the dynamics of respiratory diseases in EU/EEA countries. Submitting projections does require some basic familiarity with the GitHub platform since we use simple repository fork and pull requests. If you are comfortable with GitHub, you can easily join the Hub by submitting your projections following the instructions provided [here](https://github.com/european-modelling-hubs/ari-forecast-hub/wiki/Submitting-using-GitHub-Website).

Nonetheless, we are committed to fostering an open and inclusive modeling community and do not want technicalities to deter anyone from joining us. If you are unsure about how to initiate the submission process on GitHub, please do not hesitate to contact us at the following email address: [European.Modelling.Hub@ecdc.europa.eu](mailto:European.Modelling.Hub@ecdc.europa.eu). We are more than willing to assist you, guide you through the submission process, and provide the necessary support to help you make your contribution.
